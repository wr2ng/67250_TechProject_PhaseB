Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools, emerging technologies, and human imagination shape the future of artistic expression.

Milestones

  • 2020: MonoMuse opens its doors to the public with its inaugural exhibition, "Digital Dreams."
  • 2021: First VR art exhibition — attracting over 50,000 online visitors.
  • 2022: STEAM education program launched in collaboration with local schools.
  • 2023: New wing for interactive installations and digital art opens.
